Lauren Hagger

I was born in Benoni, South Africa in 1994. I studied Fine Arts at the university of Pretoria and graduated in 2018. I have been living and teaching in Vietnam since 2019, while trying to focus on travelling and expanding my portfolio. I look forward to having you on this journey with me.
